## 🎨 What Changed

### Visual Design
- **Clean, flat design** - Removed gradients and heavy shadows
- **Pure dark backgrounds** - Deep blacks (#0a0a0a, #121212, #1a1a1a)
- **Subtle borders** - Minimal gray borders for definition
- **Modern spacing** - Consistent padding and gaps
- **Simplified components** - Cards, buttons, and forms redesigned

### Color Palette
```
Backgrounds:
  • Body:    #0a0a0a (pure dark)
  • Shell:   #121212 (slightly lighter)
  • Surface: #1a1a1a (cards/panels)
  • Soft:    #1f1f1f (hover states)

Text:
  • Main:   #e8e8e8 (primary)
  • Muted:  #a0a0a0 (secondary)
  • Soft:   #707070 (tertiary)

Accents:
  • Primary: #3b82f6 (blue)
  • Hover:   #60a5fa (lighter blue)

Borders:
  • Subtle:  #222222
  • Soft:    #2a2a2a
  • Strong:  #333333
```
